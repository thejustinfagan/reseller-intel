## 2. BUYER PERSONAS (5-7 Truck Parts Supplier Targets)

### Persona 1: "Skip" – Fleet Maintenance Manager
**Target Business Type:** Multi-location independent fleet with 15-50 trucks

- **Profile:** Owns or manages 2-5 fleet maintenance locations. Oversees technicians, parts inventory, compliance. Makes purchasing decisions with owner approval.
- **Business Characteristics:**
  - Annual revenue: $2M-$10M
  - Service areas: Tire repair, general truck maintenance, DOT compliance
  - Typical locations: Kenworth, Cummins, general truck shops
  - Decision-making: Cost per service unit, JIT inventory, OEM relationships
  
- **Pain Points:**
  - Inconsistent parts availability from suppliers
  - Managing inventory across multiple locations
  - Keeping up with OEM part number changes (Cummins, Freightliner, Kenworth variations)
  - Driver downtime = lost revenue
  - Quality control across locations
  
- **Goals:**
  - Reduce parts lead time from 5-7 days to 24-48 hours
  - Single supplier for multi-location needs
  - Better pricing for volume commitments
  - Technical support for warranty/OEM issues
  
- **Decision Criteria:**
  - Same-day delivery or local warehouse
  - Multi-brand parts coverage (Cummins, Freightliner, Kenworth, Mack, Volvo)
  - Account management/dedicated contact
  - Credit terms (NET 30-60)
  - Loyalty discounts
  
- **Geographic Pattern:** Clusters in Louisville/Lexington, Cincinnati, Indianapolis (major trucking hubs). High concentration near I-75, I-71, I-64 corridors.

- **Database Estimate:** ~120 in KY+IN+OH+TN matching "multi-service truck shops + Cummins/OEM dealers"
- **Reality Estimate:** 200-300 (database capture ~40-60%)
- **Target Priority:** HIGH – Highest lifetime value

### 5.4 Deduplication Strategy

**Challenge:** Existing DB has ~41K records with data quality issues. New scrapes will have overlaps.

**Dedup Process:**

1. **Exact Match (First Pass):**
   - Normalized company name + ZIP code
   - High confidence matches (eliminate 20-30%)

2. **Fuzzy Match (Second Pass):**
   - Levenshtein distance on normalized name
   - Same address (city + street)
   - Phone number match
   - Eliminate ~10-15%

3. **Manual Review (Sample):**
   - Review 5% of flagged matches for accuracy
   - Adjust thresholds based on findings
   - Quality control

4. **Flag Multi-Source:**
   - Companies appearing in 2+ sources (OEM + Directory) = high confidence
   - Keep both sources; note in "source" field for CRM

---

### 5.5 Avoid Duplicates: Best Practices

1. **Maintain Source Field:**
   - Tag each record: "Cummins Dealer Locator," "Google Maps," "YP Scrape," etc.
   - Enables dedup audit trail

2. **Use Unique Fingerprints:**
   - Hash of (normalized_name + city + zip + phone)
   - Check before insert

3. **Periodic Dedup Runs:**
   - Monthly: new records vs. existing
   - Quarterly: full database dedup (data quality improves)

4. **Keep Audit Log:**
   - Record every dedup decision
   - Enables rollback if thresholds wrong
